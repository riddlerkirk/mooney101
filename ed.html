Ardua Non Timeo

We stand today to face tomorrow Through winds of change our candles glow We hold our light wherever we may go Ardua Non Timeo

We share the courage of our brothers We feel their passion in our blood We live our lives that we may show We bear the image of our God

We live our years in joy and sorrow We go with love our voices hear The bond we share is St. John Bosco And live like him, we shall not fear

We are the leaders of tomorrow We are the promise of our land Our hearts are filled The spirits yearns to grow Through the years we will stand

We are all brothers in Don Bosco We serve and lead until the end Pray the flame may glow Through a storm may blow Let the whole world know Ardua Non Timeo
